School and College

Quantum Cosmo School and College (QCSC), Bandarban is a residential school managed by Quantum foundation, Lama, Bandarban. QCSC started its journey on the 1st January, 2001. The college today is one of the most prestigious institutions in the country with around 3000 students from Nursery to A level and HSC.
