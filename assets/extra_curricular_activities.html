Extra Curricular Activities

Quantum Cosmo School has been standing first for the last five years (2015-2019) in National Children Assembly and Parade. This event is organized in Bangabandhu National Stadium on 26 March every year on eve of Independence Day. More than 200 prestigious educational institutes and children organizations participate in this competition from around the country including Dhaka. Generally, the teams performing well are always on top of the competition.


Yoga

Practice Makes a Man Perfect First thing to do in the morning is- exercise. One and half hour practice every morning is compulsory for those in the parade, display, band or any other sports teams. Our Quanta's is an active participant in two teams, display and juniorparade team. He went to Lama Upazilla for the first time in his life as member of Victory Day Parade and Display Teamlast year. As usual, Quantum Cosmo School secured first position in both parade and display events. Quanta's still recalls the shiver of joy, along with his fellow team members, when their captain received the award from the chief guest.


Meditation

School begins with meditation. As a result, Quanta's and others can concentrate properly during the class. It also sharpens their memory.

Event Segment

Library

‘HIKMAN cmapus have a library her name” ABDULLAH ABU SAYEED ‘Abdullah Abu Sayeed Library’ was inaugurated in Quantum Cosmo School in 2006. Back then junior and High school classes were conducted in the same place together. Later, as High school was shifted to ‘HIKMAN”, there are two libraries in Cosmo School now. As of December, 2018, there are 13,176 books in these two libraries.
