Play Ground

Beginning: On March 28, 2012 a trainer from Bangladesh Athletics Federation went to Lama and started his training with nine Quantas. This is how Cosmo School Athletics began its journey.


Too many players but a tiny field. Considering such reality in the past, it was a well thought out decision to select a sport that can be easily played in a limited space. Thus Handball began in Cosmo School.


Quantum Cosmo School and College became the champion for the fourth time in handball event in the 46th Summer National School and Madrasa Sports Competition-2017. The final match of the event was held in Jessore where champions of Golap, Padma and Champa regions competed with the unbeaten Quantas. Quantum Cosmo School and College won by a huge margin of 44-4 against former National Champions Fulkuri Islamic Academy from Champa region. The goal difference has set a new record in the finals of handball event in National School and Madrasa Sports Competition.
