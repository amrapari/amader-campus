Technological Skill

The school facilitates extensive reading, computer training, and scientific research so that the students can grow into well-educated and skilled human resource.


Computer Lab

Despite being situated in a remote area, this school has several computer labs with 60 computers where students can train themselves in various fields of information technology.

Quantas are learning networking in addition to basic computer training


Science Lab and Science Club

Along with having science lab and science clubs, Cosmo School has other arrangements to turn students into experts on science and technology. These programs certainly paid off. One Quanta from Quantum Cosmo School participated in the ‘National Science and Technology Week’ in 2017. His invention, ‘Auto Irrigation System’, brought him the first prize in district and sub-district level as well as second prize in divisional level.


National Science Fair

Quantas of Cosmo School and College won all the top three positions in sub-district level in National Science Fair-2018.
