Quantum Cosmo School was established to provide sound education to children in deprived communities. Starting with only 7 students in the elementary class in 2002, the school has successfully been adding higher classes each passing year. Today, Quantum Cosmo School provides secondary and intermediate level education. Students from this school have successfully passed admission tests to pursue higher education in renowned universities around the country, including Government Medical, BUET, CUET, and Dhaka, Chittagong and Rajshahi University. From 2011 to 2020, 127 students passed higher secondary level from Cosmo school and almost all of them successfully passed admission tests in the following public universities to pursue their higher education. In 2018, 10 students got accepted in Dhaka University, 12 in Chittagong University, 1 in BUET, 1 in CHUET, and 3 in Habiganj, Jamalpur and Rangpur medical college. In 2019, 3 more students got accepted in medical college. 10 more students in Dhaka University and 18 in Chittagong University in total added up to 36 students in public universities. In 2020, 30 students in total got accepted into public universities. It is important to note that all the students (quantas) from Cosmo School prepared for their undergrad admission tests from the school campus at Quantamom, without relying on any conventional coaching centers, additional tuitions, etc.
